FAQ

Questions & Answers

How do I pay for my deliveries?

You can pay using our in-app wallet, debit cards, or bank transfers. Business users can also opt for monthly invoicing.

Are there any hidden charges?

No. The price you see when placing a request is the final price you pay, provided the delivery details don't change.

What happens if a delivery is delayed?

We pride ourselves on speed, but if a delay occurs due to traffic or weather, you'll be notified via the app immediately with real-time updates.

Is my package insured during transit?

Yes, all deliveries on the Business and Enterprise tiers are automatically covered by our goods-in-transit insurance for up to ₦100,000.

Can I schedule recurring deliveries?

Absolutely! The Business and Enterprise plans allow you to set up daily, weekly, or custom recurring delivery schedules through your dashboard.

How do I get discounts for bulk shipments?

Our Enterprise plan offers volume-based discounts. If you're shipping more than 200 packages a month, contact our sales team for a custom quote.
